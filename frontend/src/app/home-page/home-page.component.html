About Us

At Millennium Nails, we are dedicated to providing you with relaxing and enjoyable nail care services and experienced by our trained staff.

We assure that customers' cleanliness and safety are our utmost importance.

  • Buffers, files, and pumice are used only once, and then discarded.
  • All pedicure procedures are performed with liner protection.
  • Our tools, equipment, and electrical instruments are always thoroughly cleaned and subjected to an approved sanitizing and disinfecting process before being reused.
